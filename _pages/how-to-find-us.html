We're hosting our upcoming events at Acorn Insurance, on the fourth floor of the Atlantic Pavilion at Albert Dock — one of Liverpool's most recognisable waterfront locations.

📍 Location Map

Open in Google Maps

🚆 By Train

The nearest train station is James Street (approx. 11-minute walk).

You can also use:

  • Liverpool Lime Street (mainline station – approx. 15-minute walk or short taxi ride)
  • Liverpool Central (approx. 15-minute walk)

From James Street:

Exit the station and head towards the waterfront → follow signs for Albert Dock → the Atlantic Pavilion is on the dockside. For evening meetups, use the side door (not the main arched entrance) — see On Arrival below.

🚌 By Bus

Several buses stop near Albert Dock and the Pier Head area from Liverpool city centre.

You can plan your journey using:

  • Merseytravel Journey Planner
  • Google Maps

🚗 By Car

The recommended car park is Royal Albert Dock Parking, directly adjacent to the venue at Royal Albert Dock, Gower St, Liverpool L3 4AS.

For more space, Liverpool ONE is the largest underground car park nearby and is a short walk from Albert Dock.

We recommend allowing extra time for parking during busy periods.

🚲 By Bike

Albert Dock is easily accessible via Liverpool's waterfront cycle routes. Bike racks are available in the Albert Dock area.

🏢 On Arrival

Important — evening entry:

Reception closes at 5pm, so the main Atlantic Pavilion entrance is not available for our meetups. Please use the side door marked in red on the photo below — an organiser will be there to let people in.

Side entrance — look for the door circled in red

Once inside, take the lift to Floor 4 for Acorn Insurance.

Arriving late? If there is no one at the side door when you arrive, contact an organiser via our Meetup group messages and we will come and let you in.
